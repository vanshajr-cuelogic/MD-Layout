CLINTON PHILLIPS

Founder & CEO

Clinton Phillips previously founded Aspen Back & Body in 2005, a front-runner in non-surgical rehabilitation for spine problems. The clinic helped 1,900 people avoid spine surgery and had over 40 of the Forbes 400 families as clients. Clinton also served as the Vice President for the Laser Spine Institute, a national leader in minimally invasive surgery, and he and his wife own the patent and business IdealStretch. He has been featured on CBS, ABC, Forbes, Aspen Peak, Town & Country, etc., and manages health matters for several royal families and U.S. leaders.

BABAK MOVASSAGHI,
PHD

President of 2nd.MD International

Babak Movassaghi, PhD, has over 12 years of scientific healthcare experience from Phillips Healthcare, where he led a team of clinical scientists to establish Phillips as the market leader in cardiac care. He has won various awards, holds 43 patents, has authored/co-authored 3 books, has over 50 peer-reviewed publications and is a judge and mentor for the MIT 100K competition. He holds an MS in Quantum Physics from Heinrich-Heine University, a PhD in Biomedical Engineering from the University of Utrecht and an MBA from MIT Sloan School of Management.

LORIE CLEMENTS, MBA

COO

Lorie Clements is a financial and business management executive with nearly fifteen years of international and domestic experience. Lorie has served as President of a Texas wide services company, as CFO securing investment capital for solar energy power plants, and Vice President roles for business development, sales, and marketing at both small and large companies domestically and abroad. Lorie holds an MBA with honors from Rice University’s, Jones School of Business and a BBA of Finance, also with honors, from the Red McCombs School of Business at the University of Texas in Austin.

JEFF A. TANGNEY

Advisor

Jeff Tangney is the founder and CEO of Doximity, Inc. He co-founded Epocrates Inc. in 1998 and is also an Advisor at Rock Health. Jeff has over 15 years of healthcare and pharmaceutical experience, and is well versed in marketing strategy as well as business development. He graduated summa cum laude from the Concordia University of Wisconsin, with a Bachelor of Science in Economics and earned an MBA from Stanford University’s Graduate School of Business.

JASON MELTON

CFO

Jason has served in a number of senior executive roles including CEO, COO, and CFO. Jason has also led multi-party engagements as a Managing Director with the Ashcroft Group on behalf of numerous Fortune 500 companies. His executive responsibilities have spanned several countries within the energy, technology, professional services, hospitality and strategic consulting sectors. Jason began his career as an investment banker at DLJ and Credit Suisse. He grew up in a state park in rural west Texas and attended Princeton University where he received an A.B. in Economics and was captain of the track and field team.

ANDRE PONTIN

EVP, Physicians

Andre Pontin has extensive expertise in business development, strategic planning, operations and relationship management. Through his leadership background within the financial industry, he has many years of experience managing multiple high profile accounts. Andre also served as Treasurer of the Rotary Club of Snowmass Village in Colorado and has a Graduate Diploma in Banking from the University of Colorado.

DWAYNE BOHAC

Advisor

Dwayne Bohac has a deep background in Benefits and Human Resources. He is a 12 year member of the Texas Legislature and currently works as a Producer with Houston-based Dean & Draper Insurance Agency, LLC. He is experienced and well versed in Texas’ Worker’s Comp law, and previously worked as District Manager and HR Product Specialist for Automatic Data Processing (ADP), supporting the entire southern region of the U.S. and Puerto Rico.

KAREN MORVAN

Marketing Guru

Karen Morvan is an award-winning graphic designer who has experience in marketing communications and brand strategy. Karen holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the University of Louisiana at Lafayette where she was nominated for Visual Arts Outstanding Graduate and an MPS in Branding from the School of Visual Arts in New York City.
